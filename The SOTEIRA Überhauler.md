The SOTEIRA Überhauler is an abnormally large colony ship originally manufactured by **[[Propulsion Laboratories|PropLAB]]** but has since been retrofitted as a cruise ship with self-sustaining services for up to 7,700 passengers and staff operated by **[[SOTEIRA]]**. 

# Purpose

The Überhauler's primary directive is to acquire passengers and clientele, who will become, whether they like it or not, live test subjects for **[[Peregrine Foundation|The Company's]]** various subsidiaries
