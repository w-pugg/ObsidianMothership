# 1. Stats
PCs have four Stats.
- **Strength**
- **Speed**
- **Intellect**
- **Combat**
>Roll 2d10 ( `dice: 2d10`) + 25 and record the result for each stat.
>*Stats represent aptitude in the particular attribute while acting under pressure.* 
# 2. Saves
PCs have three Saves.
- **Sanity**
- **Fear**
- **Body**
>Roll 2d10 ( `dice: 2d10`) + 10 and record the result for each save.
>*Saves represent resistance to trauma and a variety of dangers.*
# 3. Classes
PCs may choose between four classes.
- **Marine**
- **Android**
- **Scientist**
- **Teamster**
>Mark the chosen class. Alter stats and saves accordingly.
# 4. Health
PCs mark *wounds* when their health reaches zero.
>Roll 1d10 ( `dice: 1d10`) + 10 and record the result for Maximum Health. Mark Wounds as 2, or adjust given a class bonus.
# 5. Stress
PCs current stress and minimum stress both begin at 2.

# 7. Skills
Each class has pre-assigned relevant skills, however PCs may also choose a number of bonus skills.
>To take a skill, you must have *at least one* prerequisite first.
>Mark all allocated and bonus skills.
# 8. Loadout, Trinket, & Patch
PCs begin with a basic loadout, a trinket, and a patch, decided by rolling on tables.
