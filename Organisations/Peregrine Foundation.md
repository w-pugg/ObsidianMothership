Peregrine Foundation is a private equity firm founded on Mars by a group of wealthy investment bankers aboard the Colony Ship Peregrine.

Despite arguably futile attempts to audit and regulate, the Peregrine Foundation has the funds and the connections to effortlessly brush off such "senseless bureaucracy".
