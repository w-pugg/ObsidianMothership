The SOTEIRA is an abnormally large colony ship originally manufactured by **[[Propulsion Laboratories|PropLAB]]** but has since been retrofitted as luxury cruise ship with self-sustaining services for up to 7,700 passengers serving as the sole experience provided by **[[SOTEIRA]]**, from which it is named. 

---
# Purpose

The SOTEIRA’s primary directive is to harbour **[[Peregrine Foundation|The Company]]**‘s various subsidiaries providing the space and resources to carry out experimental testing on innovative new products.
